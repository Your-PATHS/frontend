YourPATHS

A helping hand for those in need

Your PATHS is a Phoenix-based organization dedicated to helping individuals whose lives have been impacted by domestic trafficking and commercial sexual exploitation. We're here to help you overcome whatever obstacles stand in the way of the bright future that you deserve!

Always remember: you are NOT alone! Many of us have made this journey before you, and many will make it after!

Your PATHS professionals help our members with housing, mental health, education, and potential careers. Staff members encourages clients to become empowered to take a new direction towards a positive path.

Housing

We can help find you somewhere safe to stay regardless of your financial situation.

Mental Health

It's essential to take care of your mental health just as much as your physical health. We can connect you with local mental health professionals that specialize in treating sexual trauma.

Education

Feeling left behind in today's economy? It's never too late to seek an education! We can help you find a school that suits your talents and interests.

Careers

Looking for a job? We can help you start a new career on a path that brings you fulfillment and opportunity.
